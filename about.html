About the firm

Komath & Associates is a distinguished law firm based in Kochi, Kerala, known for delivering exceptional legal services for around two decades.With a team of highly experienced and dedicated professionals, we provide comprehensive legal solutions to individuals and businesses alike. Our tagline, "Brief & forget your woes," reflects our dedication to providing efficient and effective solutions to your legal challenges.

Our firm specializes in various areas of law, including Writs, Criminal & Civil Jurisdiction, and Family matters. We have a proven track record of successfully handling complex cases and achieving favorable outcomes for our clients.

We believe in the power of knowledge and continuously stay updated with the latest developments in the legal field. This enables us to provide our clients with accurate and up-to-date legal advice, along with effective contract drafting and dispute resolution services.
Our firm also recognizes the importance of social responsibility and actively supports initiatives that promote equal rights and representation. We strive to make a positive impact on society and contribute to the betterment of the legal system.
